José Ignacio Meloni Sferco, born in Buenos Aires in 1992, is a self-taught artist-designer who creates handmade contemporary furniture and objects. He works with aluminum, brass, glass, marble, and semi-precious stones, casting and sculpting each material into collectible pieces—ranging from unique works to limited editions.

Blending ancient techniques such as metal casting with modern technologies like 3D modeling and 3D printing, his practice preserves the essence of traditional craftsmanship while using technology to explore new possibilities. Inspired by geological landscapes and jewelry-like forms, his designs explore the dialogue between the organic and the geometric—each piece fully conceived and shaped in his workshop.
